Company Overview

Mission

Lydian Agent is revolutionizing healthcare delivery through quantum-enhanced AI systems. Our platform combines 7 autonomous AI agents with IBM Quantum computing to optimize clinical decision-making, resource allocation, and patient monitoring at unprecedented scale.

One-Line Description

"World's first quantum-enhanced healthcare AI platform with 7 autonomous agents processing 10,000+ patient vitals per second."

Key Metrics & Achievements

  • 82% reduction in clinical decision time
  • 35% improvement in resource utilization vs classical algorithms
  • 10,000+ patients monitored simultaneously in real-time
  • 99.3% anomaly detection accuracy
  • Deployed at 12 major hospital systems
  • SOC 2 Type II and HITRUST certified
  • Processing 50M+ vital signs daily
