Company Culture

At Lydian Agent, we're building the future of healthcare AI. Our culture is founded on:

  • Patient First: Every decision we make considers patient safety and outcomes above all else.
  • Scientific Rigor: We validate our AI models against clinical gold standards, publish peer-reviewed research, and welcome external audits.
  • Innovation with Responsibility: We push technological boundaries while maintaining unwavering commitment to privacy, security, and regulatory compliance.
  • Diverse Perspectives: Our team includes clinicians, engineers, data scientists, and healthcare administrators from 15+ countries.
  • Continuous Learning: Annual education budget, conference attendance, certifications, and dedicated research time for all engineers.
  • Work-Life Balance: Flexible remote work, unlimited PTO, comprehensive health benefits, and generous parental leave.
